## Hi, I'm Neeraj 👋

I'm a systems engineer fascinated by what happens *before* the OS boots — the firmware, the silicon, the power sequencing, the interconnects, and all the invisible choreography that makes modern processors work.

This blog is my lab notebook. I write about:

- **ARM Architecture** — Neoverse, Cortex-M, SoC design
- **Firmware & Boot** — SCP, TF-A, UEFI, secure boot chains
- **Silicon Internals** — Interconnects (CMN-700), power domains, PLLs, DVFS
- **Hands-on Experiments** — Building and booting on FVPs, QEMU, real hardware

Everything here is written in a conversational tone because I believe complex topics deserve clear explanations, not jargon walls.
